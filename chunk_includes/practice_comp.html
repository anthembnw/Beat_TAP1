Welcome!

Thank you for participating in this study!


In this study, you will be reading sentences! The task is roughly 25 minutes long. Your task is to answer questions about the sentence you just read. You will use your mouse to choose the answer you think is best on your screen.

You will start by reading a few sentences to practice. There are 3 sentences in this practice introduction.


Some tips and reminders:
  • Respond as quickly as you can.
  • You will not be able to pause the study after starting! So please give yourself 30-40 minutes of uninterrupted time!
  • Make sure to pay attention to the sentences too. There will be comprehension questions about the content of the sentences.

Comprehension Check:

 1. What is your task?



2. How long will the task last?



3. How many sentences will I read in the practice introduction?



4. Is your screen in Full screen mode?



Then press Enter to continue!
